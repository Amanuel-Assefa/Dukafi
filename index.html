About DukaCredit

DukaCredit, is a digital ledger app designed for small business owners to manage credit and payment transactions digitally. It helps merchants keep track of receivables and payables, send collection reminders via SMS and Telegram, and access reports and account statements.

The platform supports multiple languages and ensures data security through online and local backups. Additionally, DukaCredit offers a web-based platform for merchants to manage accounts, invoices, payments, and customers. It also provides features like transactions, billing, stock management, and business loans. .
